Recently released iMol version 0.25 fixes some of the uncomplete features and bugs. It provides very nice looking display functions like solid ribbon and probably the most beautiful Molecular Viewer for OS/X. Now I can recommend it to my friends doing analysis on protein structures. The author said that after a period of stagnation he continues working on iMol thanks to numerous encouraging comments.
